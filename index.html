About me

Hi, I’m Beatriz.

This site has the purpose of showing my professional side, how I get excited and involved in my projects.

Multidisciplinary and result-oriented, university student of marketing with a strong background in marketing and communication, I love challenges and especially to see the result of my work reflected in the brands’ profits.

I’m a Communication, Marketing, Public Relations and Advertising Technician by EPAD – Escola Profissional de Artes, Tecnologias e Desporto and I’m currently attending a degree in Marketing at IPS – Instituto Politécnico de Setúbal.

I’m currently working as a visual communication consultant.

I consider myself a very creative person and as such, everything that allows me to get out of the box is welcome.
I love to go to the cinema, go out with friends and visit new places (especially if it involves new tastes).

There is no more B2B or B2C. For me, it’s H2H: Human to Human.
