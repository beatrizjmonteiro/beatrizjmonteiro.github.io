As my mother usually says, I am a young woman who cannot sit still and is always “inventing”, qualities that I consider indispensable in the path I intend to follow professionally. 

Today, I’m publishing with the intention of showing you a project that I started on behalf of my father, José Monteiro. Since I was very young I have seen my father in the area of carpentry and construction, I even have a special memory of the day he built me a dollhouse (the house was bigger than me), such was the emotion of a 5 year old child. 

I have always motivated my father to have his own business but life has not yet given him that breakthrough. Inspired, I decided to develop part of the visual communication, i.e. the logo and its respective application, giving life and motivating my father, thanking him for the wonderful memories.
